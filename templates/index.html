Introduction of project:

With the growing popularity of restaurant review websites and social media platforms, customers can easily share their experiences and opinions about a particular restaurant with the world. However, reading through hundreds or even thousands of reviews to determine whether a restaurant is worth visiting can be a time-consuming and overwhelming task.

This is where natural language processing (NLP) using machine learning (ML) techniques can be applied to analyze restaurant reviews and provide a sentiment score indicating whether a review is positive or negative.

What is Sentiment analysis?

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, is a NLP technique used to extract subjective information from text. It involves analyzing text data to determine the sentiment or opinion expressed by the writer, which can be positive, negative, or neutral.

Sentiment analysis can be applied to a wide range of text data, including social media posts, product reviews, customer feedback, news articles, and more. The goal of sentiment analysis is to automatically classify the sentiment of a piece of text, without the need for human intervention.
